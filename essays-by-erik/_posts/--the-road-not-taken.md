> Two roads diverged in a wood, and I—  
> I took the one less traveled by,  
> And that has made all the difference.
>
> — [Robert Frost, The Road Not Taken](https://www.poetryfoundation.org/poems/44272/the-road-not-taken){:target="_blank"}

We're incongruent, revisionist creatures, us humans. You. Me. Everyone.

Read the full poem linked above. I'm guilty of having cited these lines to make a profound and poignant statement about the need to choose boldly or take the high road.

> ...Had worn them really about the same...  
> ...And both that morning equally lay...  
> I shall be telling this with a sigh
